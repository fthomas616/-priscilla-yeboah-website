Live Property Search Coming Soon

This page will display live property listings from Bright MLS once the integration is complete. All properties will be searchable by location, price, bedrooms, and more.

📋 To activate live property search:

  1. Log into your Bright MLS account at BrightMLS.com
  2. Click Account dropdown (top right) → Accounts & Settings
  3. Navigate to IDX and VOW Licensing Request
  4. Select "Framed Solution" or "IDX Search Link" option
  5. Sign digital agreement (if prompted)
  6. Copy the provided iframe URL and send it to your developer

Cost: Approximately $10/month for live MLS data access

Integration time: 5 minutes once URL is provided (2-line code update)

In the meantime, feel free to contact Priscilla directly for personalized property searches and recommendations.
